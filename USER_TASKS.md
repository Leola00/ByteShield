# SpotMe – Core User Tasks

**Princess Nourah University – Parking System**

These are the top 5 most important and frequent tasks users will perform with SpotMe.

---

## 1. Search for available parking spaces

Users can use SpotMe to find **nearby available parking spaces** within the university campus.

---

## 2. Select the parking area or campus location

Users can choose their **current location** or the **building they are heading to** so the system can suggest the closest parking area.

---

## 3. Navigate to the selected parking spot

The system provides **directions** that guide users from their current position to the available parking space.

---

## 4. Save the location of the parked car

Users can **save their car’s location** after parking so they can find it later using the “Find My Car” feature.

---

## 5. Find the parked car

After saving a location, users can open **“Find My Car”** to locate their vehicle and get **directions back to it**.
